Embrace the chaos.

I move between two distinct creative frequencies. One is the pulse of a production set — a space defined by momentum, collaboration, and the beautiful mess of bringing a vision to life. The other is the sanctuary of my ceramic studio, where the world narrows down to the shape of clay under my hands.

I’ve learned that both require the same thing: presence. I find my balance in the contrast between these two worlds, each sharpening the other.

Visual Designer & Executive Producer

At Arki Pictures, I hold the framework for our storytelling together. As an Executive Producer, I manage the pulse of our productions, navigating the complexities of the set to ensure every project remains rooted in its human core. Beyond the set, I serve as our lead visual designer — shaping the brand identity, graphics, and digital spaces that define who we are. I believe in calm execution and intentional structure, ensuring our story is felt as deeply as it is seen.

Ceramic Artist & Maker

NAN STUDIO is where I explore the relationship between object and space. My practice is grounded in minimalism, creating functional ceramics that balance clean, intentional lines with small, unexpected human touches. I view these objects as an invitation to slow down — reclaiming the quiet moments of the day through the simple act of holding something made by hand.
